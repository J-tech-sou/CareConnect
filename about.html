About Us

Who we are

CareConnect is a Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) dedicated to serving all Adults living with Autism in Nigeria and Africa, supporting them to live productive and fulfilling lives, irrespective of race, religion, income levels and gender.

According to data released by the CDC in 2020, autism effects just over 2% or about 5.5 million of adults aged 18 to 84 nationwide.

Our Mission, Vision & Values

MISSION

CareConnect aim to offer comprehensive and tailored care for adults with autism through the application of evidenced-based Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) therapy, raise awareness about the challenges they face and promote policies that support their inclusion and well-being.

VISION

CareConnect vision is to create a world where adults with autsim are empowered, supported, and celebrated for their unique qualities, where they have access to the resources they need to live fulfilling lives, and where society recognizes and values their contributions.
